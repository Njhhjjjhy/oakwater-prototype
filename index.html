01 Investment focus

We believe there are three critical and irrepressible industries that will impact our world of tomorrow — and offer the greatest opportunities for savvy investors.

01

Artificial intelligence

A.I. will have a profound impact on our world of tomorrow, influencing nearly every aspect of society, economy and daily life.

02

Environment and sustainability

The need for sustainable practices, environmental stewardship and the challenges of climate change will shape global policies and impact every sector of society.

03

Health and lifestyle

The interplay between healthcare advancements and lifestyle choices will impact quality of life, economic productivity and social structures around the world.
