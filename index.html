Who we are

We believe there are three critical and irrepressible industries that will impact and shape our world of tomorrow.

01

Artificial Intelligence

We believe artificial intelligence, in all its diversity and applications, will have a profound impact on our world of tomorrow, influencing nearly every aspect of society, economy and daily life.

02

Environment and Sustainability

We believe that the need for sustainable practices, environmental stewardship and the challenges of climate change will continue to shape global policies and impact group and individual practices in every sector of society.

03

Health and Lifestyle

We believe that, as communities and generations continue to evolve, the interplay between healthcare advancements and lifestyle choices will impact the quality of life, economic productivity and social structures around the world.

Oakwater Capital is a Singapore-based licensed fund management company that aims to provide our investor clients with expert advice and focused investment vehicles targeting Artificial Intelligence, Environment & Sustainability and Health & Lifestyle sectors.

Our story

We also believe that these three sectors will offer some of the greatest opportunities for maximizing return to savvy investors.

For us lucky ones, we meet many good people throughout our careers – friends, colleagues, fellow professionals. Over many years, we stay in touch with those whom we grow to like, to trust, to respect and, if we are luckier still, to share similar values and visions.

Sometimes, we are blessed with the opportunity to bring such people together, for a meaningful cause and a growing platform.

The traits that we value are nothing uncommon – integrity, humility, reliability, acumen, humanity and an implacable aspiration to make a positive impact on our world… and it is this last trait that sometimes drives people to heed their conscience and take uncommon action.

Oakwater Capital is our action.
