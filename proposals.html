About

Oakwater Capital is a Singapore-based licensed fund management company that aims to provide our investor clients with expert advice and focused investment vehicles targeting Artificial Intelligence, Environment & Sustainability and Health & Lifestyle sectors.

Our story

For us lucky ones, we meet many good people throughout our careers – friends, colleagues, fellow professionals. Over many years, we stay in touch with those whom we grow to like, to trust, to respect and, if we are luckier still, to share similar values and visions.

Sometimes, we are blessed with the opportunity to bring such people together, for a meaningful cause and a growing platform.

The traits that we value are nothing uncommon – integrity, humility, reliability, acumen, humanity and an implacable aspiration to make a positive impact on our world… and it is this last trait that sometimes drives people to heed their conscience and take uncommon action.
